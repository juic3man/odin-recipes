Honey Hot Wings Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Celery sticks for serving
  • Blue cheese dressing for dipping

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix honey, hot sauce, melted butter, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the chicken wings to the bowl and toss until they are well coated.
  4. Place the wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 40-45 minutes, flipping halfway through, until crispy and cooked through.
  6. Serve with celery sticks and blue cheese dressing on the side.

Notes

For extra heat, add more hot sauce to the marinade.

Serving Suggestions

These wings are perfect for game day or any party!

Storage Instructions

Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Nutrition Information

Approximate values per serving: 400 calories, 25g protein, 20g fat, 30g carbs.

Tips

For extra crispy wings, broil them for the last 5 minutes of cooking.

Variations

You can use different sauces like BBQ or teriyaki for a different flavor.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use frozen wings? Yes, but make sure to thaw them before cooking.

Can I grill these wings instead? Absolutely! Just adjust the cooking time accordingly.

What can I serve with these wings? They pair well with fries, coleslaw, or a fresh salad.

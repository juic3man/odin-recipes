Asada Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank steak
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortillas
  • Chopped cilantro and onions for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate the flank steak in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Grill the steak over medium-high heat for about 5-7 minutes on each side.
  4. Let the steak rest for a few minutes before slicing it thinly.
  5. Warm the tortillas on the grill or in a pan.
  6. Assemble the tacos with sliced steak, cilantro, and onions.
  7. Serve with lime wedges on the side.

Notes

Feel free to add your favorite toppings like salsa, avocado, or cheese!

Serving Suggestions

These tacos pair well with a side of Mexican rice or refried beans.

Storage Instructions

Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.

Nutrition Information

Approximate values per serving: 300 calories, 20g protein, 15g fat, 30g carbs.

Tips

For extra flavor, try adding some chipotle peppers to the marinade.

Variations

You can substitute the flank steak with chicken or shrimp for a different twist.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a different cut of meat? Yes, you can use skirt steak or sirloin as alternatives.

Can I make these tacos ahead of time? Yes, you can marinate the meat a day in advance.

What toppings do you recommend? Chopped onions, cilantro, avocado, and salsa are great options.

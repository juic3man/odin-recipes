Protien Shake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop protein powder (whey or plant-based)
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Chia seeds or flaxseeds (optional)
  • Spinach or kale (optional for added nutrients)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the protein powder, banana, almond milk, and peanut butter.
  2. Add honey if you prefer a sweeter shake.
  3. Add ice cubes for a chilled shake.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. If desired, add chia seeds or flaxseeds for extra nutrition and blend again.
  6. For a green shake, add spinach or kale and blend until well mixed.
  7. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Notes

You can customize this shake by adding your favorite fruits or superfoods.

Serving Suggestions

This protein shake is perfect for breakfast, post-workout recovery, or a healthy snack.

Storage Instructions

For best taste, consume immediately. If you need to store it, keep it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours.

Nutrition Information

Approximate values per serving: 300 calories, 25g protein, 10g fat, 30g carbs.

Tips

For a thicker shake, use frozen banana or add more ice.

Variations

You can substitute almond milk with coconut water or regular milk.
